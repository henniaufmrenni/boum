boum a sleek music app for Jellyfin media servers.

Some of the features of boum include:
<li>ability to download albums</li>
<li>offline view, to only see your downloaded media</li>
<li>cross navigation e.g. navigate from albums to the artist from the player to album / artist</li>
<li>basic queue management</li>
<li>sleeptimer</li>
<li>ability to start a scan for new music on the Jellyfin server</li>
<li>saving the queue to pick up where you left off (for all the audiobook listeners out there)</li>
<li>and more..</li>

About the name:
boum was the "cool" french word in the 80s for a party.
